Prayer & Coffee

Prayer & Coffee

A weekly gathering to praise God and pray together

TUESDAY 10:30 AM

What We Do

This weekly gathering is a time to give thanks to God, praising Him for His faithfulness, goodness, and love towards us.

It's also a chance to seek His guidance and bring our prayer requests for families, friends, cities, and nations.

Following the gathering, we have a time of fellowship with free coffee, tea, and cakes to enjoy.

Everyone is welcome!

Prayer Breakfast

We also host a Prayer Breakfast on the first Saturday of each month.

  • Breakfast: 9:30 AM
  • Prayer: 10:30 AM

This gathering offers a chance to enjoy breakfast, worship, share encouragement, and pray for our mission in the community, especially in our harvest zones we are reaching out to.
