Youth Group

Youth Group

A fun, safe, and faith-filled space for young people!

When

  • Starts: 22 October 2025
  • Day: Every Wednesday
  • Time: 6:00 – 7:30 PM

Who

  • Ages: 10 – 16 years old
  • Leader: Sara-Jane Roberts

What We Do

Join us each week for a lively mix of games, music, storytelling, food, and more! Our Youth Group is a place to build friendships, explore faith, and have a great time together.

Whether you're new or a regular, you're welcome — come hang out, bring a friend, and be part of the fun!
